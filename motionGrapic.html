Motion Grapic

This series was made during my off-hours as a UIUX designer. I used Adobe Illustrator to fill textures, and Photoshop to export video clip.

During the process, making the animation loop infinite is the most fun part. I like the noodles in the upper right corner the most. It makes us want to eat the noodles but never be able to do that.

I also like to illustrate daily objects in geometric figures. They become even more unique after applying the materials.
