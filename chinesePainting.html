Chinese painting

Art mediums: Prepared chinese ink, china paper.

One of my majors in the university is the Department of Calligraphy and Painting. We spent a lot of time learning the brushwork and rendering skills of Chinese painting.

The small river on the mountain behind my hometown is where I used to play with my neighbers in my childhood.

The split second of the stream splashes are the most difficult to express. I need to capture the moment with my own eyes when it splashes.
