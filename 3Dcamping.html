3D modeling Camping

I illustrated a scene where I went camping with my friends. The most difficult thing was to create a sunset atmosphere. The campsite was located beside a river bank, so a reflection of the candle lantern could be seen on the surface of the water.

Another thing that took me a lot of time was building the coal in the firepit by creating two gradients separately and combining them, to show the original color of the coal and the warm yellow light of the burning fire.

In the end, I demonstrated the bed and the light inside the tent. The air mattress and the pillows used the cloth's physical properties to present a comfortable texture. It was interesting that the air mattress kept flying away while I was adjusting the pressure of it, haha.

What I am most satisfied with is the candle lantern, every part of it including the iron chain, the glass lampshade, the candle, and the glowing flame are super fulfilling!
