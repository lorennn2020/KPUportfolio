3D modeling Camping

I want to illustrate a scene where I went camping with my friends. The most painful thing is to create a sunset atmosphere. I set the camp area at a river bank. You can see some reflections of bamboo lanterns on the water.

Another thing that took me a lot of time was how to make one end of the coal in the firepit maintain the original color, while the other end emits a warm yellow light. I create two gradients separately and then combined them. It finally succeeded.

What I am most satisfied with is the UCO candle lantern. The iron chain, the glass lampshade, the candle inside, and the glowing flame are also super fulfilling!

In the end, I wanted to demonstrate the light inside the tent. You could see the air mattress and pillows using the cloth's physical properties. Adjusting the pressure when making an air mattress, the air bed keeps flying away, haha.
