3D modeling Anchor

I spent two weeks learning Blender on Youtube by myself. From modeling, applying textures, rendering, and lighting.

My first 3D work Anchor was completed by using the techniques I learned in the first week. The 4th picture was the first model at the early stage, it took the most time to create small triangles at the end of the hook. I was not satisfied with the proportions of this shape since there were too many unnecessary edges and the 3D model began to break. So I adjusted each part to present a better version of the anchor. For me, the best part of this work is the uneven and matte iron texture on the hook surface.
