3D modeling Anchor

I spent two weeks learning Blender on Youtube by myself. From modeling, applying textures, rendering, and lighting.

My first 3D work — Anchor. Used the techniques I learned in the first week. In the beginning, the modeling was the 4th picture. It took the most time to create the small wings at the end of the hook. There were too many unnecessary edges, and the 3D model began to break. Later, I was not satisfied with the proportions of this shape, so Adjusting each part to present the cute version of the anchor. The best part of this work is the uneven, matte iron texture on the hook surface.
